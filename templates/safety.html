AIyoda - Child Safety Standards

AIyoda is committed to providing a safe and secure online environment for children. We take the safety of children very seriously and have implemented the following measures to protect them:

1. Prevention of Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation

  • We do not allow content that depicts, promotes, or facilitates child sexual abuse or exploitation.
  • We have reporting mechanisms in place for users to report any content or behavior that they believe violates our child safety standards.
  • We work with law enforcement to investigate and prosecute any individuals who attempt to exploit children through our services.

2. Addressing Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM)

  • We have a zero-tolerance policy for CSAM. Any content that depicts child sexual abuse will be immediately removed and reported to the National Center for Missing and Exploited Children (NCMEC).
  • We use technology and human review to proactively identify and remove CSAM from our services.

3. Compliance with Child Safety Laws

  • We comply with all applicable child safety laws, including the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and the Protecting Children from Internet Pornographers Act of 2011.
  • We regularly review and update our policies and procedures to ensure that we are meeting the latest child safety standards.

4. Reporting Concerns

If you have any concerns about the safety of a child on our services, please report it to us immediately. You can report concerns by contacting us at your-email@example.com.

5. Point of Contact for Child Safety Issues

For any questions or concerns regarding child safety, please contact our designated child safety agent at aiyodachad@gmail.com.

We are committed to continuously improving our child safety measures and working with parents, educators, and law enforcement to create a safer online environment for children.
